## Using Metrom's Safe app

You can use Metrom with Safe through the dedicated Safe app
[Metrom Safe App](https://safe-app.metrom.xyz).

### Getting Started

Follow these steps to add and use Metrom in your Safe account:

1. Open the [Safe app](https://app.safe.global/apps/custom).
2. Navigate to the `Apps -> My custom apps` section.
3. Enter the Metrom Safe App URL: https://safe-app.metrom.xyz.
4. Confirm and add the app.

You're all set! You can now use Metrom within your Safe account.
